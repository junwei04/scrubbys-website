FAQ

Common Questions

How much does post renovation cleaning cost in Singapore?

Post renovation cleaning with Scrubbys SG starts from $350 for a 300 to 599 sft unit, scaling up by size. Maisonettes add $50, and landed or commercial units require a site inspection. WhatsApp 90716978 with your home size for an exact quote.

How much does end of tenancy cleaning cost?

End of tenancy cleaning starts from $380 for a 300 to 599 sft unit. We clean to landlord inspection standard, covering all rooms, kitchen, and bathrooms.

Do I need to take down my curtains for curtain cleaning?

No. Scrubbys SG steam cleans curtains in place using a pole extension steam tool, so there's no need to take them down or send them out. Pricing is $20 per piece.

What is formaldehyde treatment and do I need it after renovation?

Formaldehyde is a chemical released by new furniture, paint, and renovation materials, and can affect indoor air quality for months. Our treatment includes pre and post treatment air quality readings, starting from $288.

What areas in Singapore does Scrubbys SG cover?

We cover all of Singapore, including HDB flats, condominiums, and landed properties island-wide.

How do I book a cleaning service?

WhatsApp us at 90716978. We typically reply within the hour and can usually schedule within a few days.

What equipment does Scrubbys SG use for floor cleaning?

We use the Rotowash Floor Scrub machine for deep floor cleaning, suitable for vinyl, cement, tiles, parquet, engineered timber, and marble.

Does Scrubbys SG offer discounts?

We don't offer discounts on individual services, but we do offer value bundles, combining post renovation cleaning with other services at a better combined rate than booking separately.
